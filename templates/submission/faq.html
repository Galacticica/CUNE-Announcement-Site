Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit an announcement?

Fill out the form on the submission page with your announcement details, dates, and any required slides.

What file types are accepted for slides?

Only PNG and JPG files are accepted. Please ensure your slides are in 16:9 aspect ratio (1920x1080 recommended).

How can I resize my slide if it isn't correct?

Online tools such as Canva can help you resize your slides to the correct aspect ratio. If you are looking to resize a poster to the correct aspect ratio, here is a tutorial on how to do so: Resizing a Poster

Can I submit multiple slides?

Yes, you can upload multiple slide files at once using the submission form.

How long will my announcement be displayed?

Announcements are displayed between the start and end dates you select in the form.
